Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and appointments

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially for weekend appointments. For special occasions like weddings or events, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead to ensure availability.

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, we'll conduct a consultation to understand your beauty goals, discuss your preferences, and recommend the best treatments for you. Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

Do you offer package deals or loyalty programs?

Yes! We offer various package deals for multiple services and a loyalty program for regular clients. Ask about our bridal packages, monthly membership options, and referral rewards when you visit or call.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen, so please call us to discuss your situation.
